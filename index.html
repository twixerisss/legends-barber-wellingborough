About Us

The Legend's
Experience

Located on Silver Street in the heart of Wellingborough, Legend's Barber delivers premium Turkish barbering with skill and passion.

Our expert barbers bring years of experience in precision fades, traditional hot towel shaves, and the latest modern styling techniques. Every cut is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Walk-ins are always welcome, or book online via Fresha for guaranteed availability at a time that suits you.

Whether you're looking for a sharp skin fade, a classic gentleman's cut, or a relaxing hot towel shave, our team delivers legendary results every time.
