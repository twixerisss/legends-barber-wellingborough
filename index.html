01 — The Shop

Heritage barbering, made on Silver Street.

A traditional Turkish barbershop in the heart of Wellingborough. Skilled hands, sharp blades, and one guiding rule — every man leaves looking sharper than he walked in.

NN8 · 1BQ The Chair

"A proper haircut is a quiet ceremony — sharp blade, hot towel, steady hand. No shortcuts."

Legend's Barber is a Turkish shop in the old way — precision cuts, scissor-over-comb work, skin fades blended invisible, and the kind of straight-razor finish your grandfather would recognise. Steam, towel, lather, blade.

Walk-ins welcome alongside appointments. Whether it's a quick neck-tidy on the way home or the full chair experience — cut, shave, hot towel, the works — we'll make the time. Seven days a week, every week.
